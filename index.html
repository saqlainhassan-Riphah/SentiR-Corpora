SentiR-Corpora

Corpus-Based Computational Linguistics Research Portal

About

SentiR-Corpora is a computational linguistics corpus designed for sentiment analysis, discourse studies, and NLP research. It provides structured linguistic datasets for academic and computational research purposes.
